About Me

I am a computational astrophysicist. I currently work on numerical simulations of accretion flows onto compact objects and am interested in understanding the properties of relativistic jets from accreting black hole sources.

For my doctoral thesis, I work with the HARM code, modeling the accretion flows around a black hole with general relativistic magnetohydrodynamic (GRMHD) simulations.

I did my Masters in Physics from the School of Physical Sciences of Central University of Kerala, India.

I grew up in a small village in Kerala, a southern state in India. From my highschool years, I grew a great interest in physics which I continue to pursue until today.
